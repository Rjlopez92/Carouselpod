Skipper Tips from Carousel of Conversations
🎯 Short on time? Minimum path: Clone Wars S2 E12–14 + S7 E9–12 → Rebels S3 E15–16 + S4 E1–2 + finale → All of Mando S1 & S2 → Book of Boba Fett Eps 5–7 → Mando S3. About 25 hours of pure magic!
⚠️ Book of Boba Fett warning: The show is a bit of a mixed bag, BUT Episodes 5–7 are basically Mandalorian 2.5 — you'll be lost in Season 3 without them. Trust us on this one!
🐊 Fun fact! Rotta the Hutt — rescued by Ahsoka in the 2008 Clone Wars movie — is confirmed to appear in the film (voiced by Jeremy Allen White of The Bear). Worth watching for that alone!
🦊 Zeb alert! Garazeb Orrelios from Star Wars Rebels is confirmed in the movie. His debut in Rebels S1 is a fun watch, and seeing him in live action is going to be incredible.
⚔️ Thrawn matters: Grand Admiral Thrawn is expected to be a major player. His full Rebels arc (S3–4) plus the Ahsoka series will make his movie role hit so much harder. The carousel of stories all connects!
