Our Story

About the Podcast

Carousel of Conversations was born from a shared love of Disney magic and a desire to create a space where fans could come together, share their stories, and celebrate everything that makes the Disney universe so extraordinary.

🎡 How It Started

What started as casual conversations between friends quickly grew into something bigger. We realized there were so many Disney fans out there who wanted a podcast that felt personal, warm, and genuinely passionate — not just a news recap. So we hit record, and Carousel of Conversations was born.

🎙️ What We Do

Each episode, we explore a different corner of the Disney universe — from deep-cut Imagineering history and park tips to film analysis, Disney news, and conversations with fellow fans. No topic is too big or too small if it sparks that magical feeling.

✨ Our Community

We believe Disney magic is best when shared. Our listeners aren't just an audience — they're part of the conversation. We love hearing your stories, your memories, and what makes Disney special to you.

🗓️ New Episodes Every Monday

New episodes drop every Monday morning wherever you listen to podcasts. Subscribe on Spotify or Apple Podcasts so you never miss a ride, and catch the full video versions on our YouTube channel.
